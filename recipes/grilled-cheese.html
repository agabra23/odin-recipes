A Delicious Grilled Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices of Sourdough Bread
  • Gouda, Cheddar, or any other high-quality cheeses
  • Butter
  • Optional: Cooked Bacon

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick pan to medium-low.
  2. While the pan is heating, spread butter onto 1 side of each slice of bread.
  3. Cut the cheeses into small pieces and place in a pile onto the non-buttered side of a slice of bread.
  4. Optional: Add the cooked bacon on top of cheese.
  5. Place that same slice of bread with the cheese onto the pan, butter side down.
  6. Place the other slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  7. Let the bread grill on the pan until golden brown and flip.
  8. Let the bread grill on the pan until golden brown and cheese is melted.
  9. Place the sandwich on a plate, cut diagonally, and enjoy!
