The Flawless Fried Egg

Ingredients:

  • 1 Whole Egg
  • Oil of Choice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Optional: Other Seasonings of Choice

Instructions:

  1. Drizzle about 1 Tbsp of oil onto a non-stick pan.
  2. Heat the pan on medium heat until hot.
  3. Gently crack open the egg onto the puddle of oil and let it sizzle.
  4. Let the egg cook until whites start to completely set.
  5. Once the whites are set, add salt and pepper (and other seasoning if desired) on top of the egg.
  6. If over-easy egg is desired, flip the egg and let it cook for about 1 minute.
  7. Once done, place the egg onto a plate and, most importantly, enjoy!
